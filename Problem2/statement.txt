LLD Interview Problem: Media Upload Service (Simplified)

📖 Short Problem Statement

Design a Media Upload Service that supports:

1. Image Compression

- Users upload images in various formats (JPEG, PNG).

- Compress before storage using both an old JPEG compressor (legacy) and a new PNG compressor.

- Provide a unified ImageCompressor interface. 


2. Delivery Channel Selection

- After storage, images may be delivered via CDN or Email.
- Decouple delivery logic from channels.
