- Add/Remove items
- Calculate price for cart
- Reserve inventory
- Collect Payments
- Create an order

Clearifying Questions: 
    - What type of currency I have to deal (INR/$) And their Tax Rule.
    - How many Paymet Methods are supported here.
    - Is there single warehouse or multiple Inventories are there.
    - Guest user or Only Registered user can create order ?
    - Concurrency while cart and payment Race Condition.
    
Non Functional Requirements:   
    - Consistency - Do not overlap stock.
    - Extensibility - Can add future payment Methods
    - Idempotency - Can't re submit the payment.

Entities : 
    - Product : productId, name, price
    - Inventory: productId, availableQty, reservedQty
    - Cart: cartId, userId, vector<CartProducts>, total_price
    - CartProduct: productId, price, qty
    - Order: orderId, userId, vector<OrderProduct>, orderStatus
    - OrderProduct: productId, qty, unitPrice
    - Payment: paymentId, orderId, amount, orderStatus, payment_Method
    - Address: line1, line2, city, state, pinCode
