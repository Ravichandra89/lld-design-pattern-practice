Interviewer: Design a Single-Restaurant Food Ordering System.
The restaurant wants a backend service to:

publish its menu,

let customers browse and place orders (takeaway / dine-in / delivery),

track order status (placed → confirmed → preparing → ready → completed/cancelled),

allow kitchen staff to view & update orders,

accept payment (card / cash on delivery),

notify customers about status changes.

Constraints: start with one restaurant (no multi-branch), but the system should be clean and extensible. Focus on API design, data models, concurrency (inventory/availability), and how to ensure no double-booking/overselling. Explain the components, failure modes, and major tradeoffs.
